# Discovery Stage

## Purpose

Identify product opportunities without committing to implementation.

## Inputs

- Problem observations.
- User or operator pain.
- Existing manual processes.
- Constraints from the target operating environment.

## Outputs

- Idea brief.
- Initial risk notes.
- Recommendation to proceed, hold, or discard.

## Gate

Proceed only when the idea has a clear problem, plausible user, and reason to believe the offline/on-prem constraints are acceptable.
